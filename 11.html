Aquarius

Aquarius (zodiac sign) - is the eleventh sign of the zodiac , corresponding to the ecliptic sector from 300° to 330°, counting from the point of the vernal equinox ; a fixed [ 1 ] sign of the trine - Air . Usually depicted as a man with a jug pouring water. In Western astrology, the Sun is believed to be in the sign of Aquarius from approximately January 21 to February 20 , while in Vedic astrology , it is Kumbha from February 13 to March 15. The sign of Aquarius should not be confused with the constellation Aquarius , in which the Sun currently resides from February 16 to March 12 .
