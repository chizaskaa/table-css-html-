Aries ( Latin: Aries - ram) - is the first sign of the zodiac , corresponding to the ecliptic sector from 0° to 30°, counting from the point of the vernal equinox ; the cardinal sign of the Fire trine . In Western astrology, the Sun is considered to be in the sign of Aries from approximately March 21 to April 20 , while in Vedic astrology, it is Mesha from April 14 to May 15. The sign of Aries should not be confused with the constellation Aries , in which the Sun is located from April 19 to May 13 .
