Pisces

Pisces ( Latin: Pisces ) - is the twelfth sign of the zodiac , corresponding to the ecliptic sector from 330° to 360°, counting from the point of the vernal equinox ; a female mutable sign of the trine of Water , winter. According to the coordinates of the Western zodiac, the Sun is in the sign of Pisces from approximately February 20 to March 20 , and in the Vedic tradition, it is in the sign of Mina from March 15 to April 14. The sign of Pisces should not be confused with the constellation Pisces , in which the Sun is from March 13 to April 19.
