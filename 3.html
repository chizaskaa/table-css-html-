Virgo

Virgo ( Latin: Virgo , lit.  "Inviolate") - is the sixth sign of the zodiac , corresponding to the ecliptic sector from 150° to 180°, counting from the point of the vernal equinox ; a mutable sign of the Earth trine . In Western astrology, the Sun is believed to be in the sign of Virgo from approximately August 23 to September 23 , and in Vedic astrology, from September 17 to October 15. The sign of Virgo should not be confused with the constellation Virgo , in which the Sun is from September 16 to October 30 .
