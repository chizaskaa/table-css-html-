Sagittarius

Sagittarius ( Latin: Sagittarius ) - is the ninth sign of the zodiac , corresponding to the ecliptic sector from 240° to 270°, counting from the point of the vernal equinox ; the mutable sign of the trine is Fire . According to the coordinates of Western astrology, the Sun is in the sign of Sagittarius from approximately November 23 to December 21 , and according to Vedic astrology, Dhana from December 16 to January 14. The sign of Sagittarius should not be confused with the constellation Sagittarius , in which the Sun is from December 18 to January 17.
