Leo ( Latin: Leo ) - is the fifth sign of the zodiac , corresponding to the ecliptic sector from 120° to 150°, counting from the point of the vernal equinox ; a fixed sign of the trine Fire . In Western astrology, the Sun is considered to be in the sign of Leo from approximately July 23 to August 23 , while in Vedic astrology, it is Simha from August 15 to September 15. The sign of Leo should not be confused with the constellation Leo , in which the Sun is located from August 10 to September 15 .
