Cancer

Cancer ( Latin: Cancer ) - is the fourth sign of the zodiac , corresponding to the ecliptic sector from 90° to 120°, counting from the point of the vernal equinox ; a cardinal sign of the Water trine . It rules the fourth house of the horoscope. In Western astrology, the Sun is believed to be in the sign of Cancer from approximately June 22 to July 22 , while in Vedic astrology, it is Cancer from July 17 to August 17. The sign of Cancer should not be confused with the constellation of the same name, Cancer , in which the Sun is from July 21 to August 9 .
