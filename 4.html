Capricorn

Capricorn ( Latin: Capricorn ) - is the tenth sign of the zodiac , corresponding to the ecliptic sector from 270° to 300°, counting from the point of the vernal equinox ; the cardinal sign of the trine is Earth . According to the coordinates of Western astrology, the Sun is in the sign of Capricorn from December 22 to January 20 , and according to Vedic astrology, Makara from January 14 to February 13. The sign of Capricorn should not be confused with the constellation Capricorn , in which the Sun is from January 18 to February 15 .
