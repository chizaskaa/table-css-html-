Scorpio

Scorpio - is the eighth sign of the zodiac , corresponding to the ecliptic sector from 210° to 240°, counting from the point of the vernal equinox ; a fixed sign of the trine Water . According to Western astrology, the Sun is in the sign of Scorpio from approximately October 24 to November 22. The sign of Scorpio should not be confused with the constellation Scorpio , in which the Sun is in from November 23 to 29 .
