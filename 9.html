Taurus

Taurus ( Latin: Taurus ) - is the second sign of the zodiac , corresponding to the ecliptic sector from 30° to 60°, counting from the point of the vernal equinox , spring; a fixed sign of the Earth trine . According to the Western astrological tradition, the Sun is in the sign of Taurus from approximately April 21 to May 21 , according to the Vedic tradition - Vrishabha from May 15 to June 15. The sign of Taurus should not be confused with the constellation of the same name Taurus , in which the Sun is from May 14 to June 19 . The planet that rules the sign is Venus , exalts the Moon , Mars is in exile. The triplicity rulers are Venus, Moon, Mars (according to Dorotheus of Sidon ). The first decan of Taurus is ruled by Mercury , the second by the Moon, the third by Saturn .
