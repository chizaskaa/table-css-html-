Gemini

Gemini (zodiac sign) - is the third sign of the zodiac , corresponding to the ecliptic sector from 60° to 90°, counting from the point of the vernal equinox ; spring; a mutable sign of the trine of the element of Air . In Western astrology, the Sun is considered to be in the sign of Gemini from approximately May 22 to June 21 , while in Vedic astrology , it is Mithuna from June 15 to July 17. The sign of Gemini should not be confused with the constellation of the same name, Gemini , in which the Sun is located from June 21 to July 21 . The planet that rules the sign is Mercury , Jupiter is in exile. The triplicity rulers are Saturn - diurnal, Mercury - nocturnal and Jupiter - joint (according to Dorotheus of Sidon ). The first decan of Gemini is ruled by Jupiter, the second by Mars , the third by the Sun.
